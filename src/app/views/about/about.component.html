About This Project

We exist to document, preserve, and elevate evidence of unlawful violence by ICE agents. Our goal is simple: create a trustworthy record that can support accountability efforts and be shared with civil-rights advocates, including the ACLU.

Why We’re Here

For months, community members have reported excessive force with little to no consequence. This platform helps you turn incidents into structured, verifiable documentation. Your report won’t be just a social post—it will be evidence capable of review by attorneys, investigators, and journalists.

What This Site Does

  • Collects reports describing incidents involving ICE personnel.
  • Accepts media (photos/videos) and preserves technical metadata when available.
  • Generates a structured record suitable for legal review and advocacy.
  • Facilitates referrals to civil-rights organizations where appropriate.

What To Include In Your Report

  • Date, time, and location (as exact as possible).
  • What happened, step-by-step, in your own words.
  • Descriptions of involved officers (uniforms, markings, vehicles).
  • Names and contact info for witnesses (if they consent).
  • Photos/videos, ideally originals with metadata intact.
  • Injuries or property damage (attach records if available).
  • Badge numbers, unit identifiers, or other unique details.
  • Any prior related reports or case numbers.

Safety & Privacy

  • Your submission is voluntary. Share only what you’re comfortable disclosing.
  • Consider the safety implications of naming individuals. Get consent where appropriate.
  • Uploading original files may include GPS/location data. You can choose to remove it, but original metadata can help verify authenticity.
  • Do not upload anything you were not legally allowed to record or that could endanger others without consent.

How Your Report May Be Used

Submissions may be reviewed to assess credibility and relevance. With your permission, reports and media may be shared with civil-rights organizations (including the ACLU), legal clinics, or investigative partners for the purposes of advocacy, litigation, and public accountability. We may contact you for clarification or corroboration.

Important Notices

  • No Legal Advice: This site does not provide legal advice and does not create an attorney-client relationship.
  • Lawful Use: Do not upload material you do not have the right to share.
  • Authenticity: Submissions should be truthful and accurate to the best of your knowledge.
  • Minors: If you are under 18, obtain consent from a parent/guardian or a trusted adult before submitting.

Our Commitment

We stand with communities seeking dignity and accountability. This platform is built to transform lived experience into credible, actionable records—because everyone deserves to be safe and free from unlawful violence.
